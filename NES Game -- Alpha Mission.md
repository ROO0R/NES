**Game Plot**:  
In "Alpha Mission," you're a pilot defending your planet against the aggressive alien fleet of GARTHA. The story unfolds as you navigate through various sectors, each protected by powerful bosses. Your mission is clear: to defeat the enemy, protect your home, and secure peace. The plot emphasizes heroism, skill, and the classic battle between good and evil, as you embark on a solitary quest to save your civilization from a technologically superior foe.

- - -
**What to Expect**:  
In "Alpha Mission," you will pilot your spacecraft vertically up the screen, navigating through hordes of enemies and dodging a barrage of bullets. Use the D-Pad to move your ship in all directions, ensuring you avoid enemy attacks and obstacles. The A button fires your main weapon, essential for destroying enemy ships. The B button activates special weapons, which you collect as power-ups throughout the game. These special weapons are crucial for defeating tougher enemies and bosses. You're tasked with strategizing which power-ups to collect and when to use them, offering a blend of action and tactical gameplay.

**TL;DR**: Pilot a ship, dodge, shoot enemies, collect power-ups, beat bosses.
