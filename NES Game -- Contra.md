**Game Plot**: In "Contra," players take on the roles of Bill Rizer and Lance Bean, elite soldiers tasked with stopping an alien threat known as the Red Falcon Organization from taking over Earth. The game is set in the futuristic year of 2633, where the duo must battle through various environments, from jungles to alien lairs, to defeat the alien forces and save the planet. The story unfolds through intense run-and-gun gameplay rather than cutscenes, emphasizing action over narrative.

- - -
**What to Expect**: "Contra" is a run-and-gun platformer where you'll primarily use the D-Pad to move and navigate through levels. The A button is used to jump, crucial for avoiding enemies and obstacles, while the B button fires your weapon. Your goal is to survive waves of enemies, defeat bosses, and progress through the game's stages. Each level offers different challenges, including varied enemies and terrain. The game is known for its difficulty, requiring quick reflexes and strategic use of power-ups, which can be picked up to enhance your weapon's abilities.

**TL;DR**: Navigate through levels, dodge and defeat enemies, and survive to stop an alien invasion.
