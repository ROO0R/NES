**What to Expect**: You'll be rotating and positioning falling blocks to create complete lines, which then disappear to free up space. Use the D-Pad to move the blocks left or right, and press A to rotate them. Pressing B will drop the blocks faster into place. Your goal is to prevent the blocks from stacking to the top of the screen. Itâ€™s a test of spatial awareness and quick thinking.
