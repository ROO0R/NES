**Game Controls**:
Navigate with the D-pad, A button to shoot, B button for special actions like deploying traps. Start opens the menu.

- - -
**Game Plot**: 
Set on Isla Nublar, "Jurassic Park" for the NES follows Dr. Alan Grant's efforts to escape the park. After a power outage, dinosaurs roam free, and Grant must navigate the chaos. His objectives include rescuing Hammond's grandchildren, Lex and Tim, reactivating the park's computers, and destroying Velociraptor nests with time bombs, culminating in a daring escape via the park's dock and a helipad.

- - -
**What to Expect**: 
Expect a top-down adventure where you collect dinosaur eggs and access cards to progress, armed with guns found in levels to battle Velociraptors and Dilophosaurus. Avoid the T. rex, as it can't be killed, only dodged. "Mystery boxes" throughout offer power-ups or challenges. With three lives and four continues, strategize your exploration and combat carefully for survival.

**TL;DR**:
Survive, rescue, and escape from dinosaur-infested Jurassic Park.
