**Game Plot**: In "Batman - Return of the Joker", the Dark Knight faces off against his arch-nemesis, the Joker, who has escaped from Arkham Asylum and is threatening Gotham City with a new, dangerous brand of technology. Batman must navigate through various levels, battling the Joker's henchmen, to stop the Joker's latest plan and restore peace to Gotham. The game focuses on Batman's quest to confront and defeat the Joker, without delving deeply into character backstories, instead emphasizing action and the urgency of Batman's mission.

- - -
**What to Expect**: Players take control of Batman, navigating through side-scrolling levels filled with enemies and obstacles. Using the D-Pad moves Batman left or right, while the A button makes him jump, and the B button uses Batman's gadgets or attacks. Each level requires defeating enemies, dodging traps, and solving minor puzzles to progress. Certain levels also include vehicle-based sections, adding variety to the gameplay. Players must balance offense and defense, using Batman's arsenal effectively to overcome challenges.

**TL;DR**: Navigate levels, defeat enemies, use gadgets to advance.
